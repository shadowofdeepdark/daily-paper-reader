## 摘要
本研究利用脑电图（EEG）揭示了期望如何通过双相时间机制动态地塑造视觉诱发的脑反应：早期阶段增强对预期图像成分的编码，随后在晚期阶段优先处理未预期成分。这种时间上的分离表明，大脑最初以贝叶斯的方式将感觉输入与先验期望相结合，之后转向强调令人惊讶的信息，从而同时支持高效识别与适应性模型更新。值得注意的是，这一更新过程首先增强类别层面的表征，然后再增加图像个体身份的编码。

## Abstract
This electroencephalography (EEG) study reveals how expectations dynamically shape visually evoked brain responses through a biphasic temporal mechanism: an early enhancement of encoding for expected image components, followed by a later prioritization of unexpected components. This temporal dissociation suggests that the brain initially integrates sensory input with prior expectations in a Bayesian manner before shifting to emphasize surprising information, thereby supporting both efficient recognition and adaptive model updating. Notably, this updating process first amplifies category-level representations before increasing the encoding of image identity.
